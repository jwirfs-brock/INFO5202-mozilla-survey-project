How many tech terms can do people say they can explain to a friend?

One of the questions on the Mozilla survey was:

Check all the terms below that you could explain to a friend:

  • IoT
  • Connected Devices
  • Botnet
  • Blockchain
  • RFID
  • DDOS
  • Zero Day
  • VPN
  • TOR
  • I don't know what any of these things are

This series of maps explores how people answered this question, by country. You can see the average number of terms people said they could explain, and the most/least explainable term.

Audio-Tactile Map: Top Explainable Terms

We also wanted to explore this information in a more creative way, so we made a physicalization using a micro:bit controller with the speech module, a physical map, and a speaker. A creepy computer Dalek voice reads the top explainable terms when you touch a country to complete a circuit. We chose the Dalek voice because a major theme of the Mozilla survey was fear and anxiety about the future of technology. (Also, micro:bit is created by the BBC.)
